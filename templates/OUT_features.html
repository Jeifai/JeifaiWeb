Here condensed all Jeifai's features. More on the way!



1 ~ Home Dashboard

A quick look to monitor your main KPIs, how your keywords are performing, as well as useful insights about your targets. Defintely powerful!



2 ~ Targets suggestion

Pick companies from our list of alternatively choose new ones! This is pretty cool: in no time you can have full control of what is happening around you.



3 ~ Keywords management

Our interface allows you to quickly manage all your keywords from a single place. Easily apply a keyword to a target or to all of them, as well as getting rid of the non relevant ones.



4 ~ Advanced filters

Filters greatly enhance your experience. Our filters are smart, you can apply them to any columns, allowing you to focus only on the information you need.



5 ~ Notifications

Rapidity is a must have skill nowdays. As soon as a keyword matches a target you get notified by email. You will be able to take actions faster and before anyone else.



6 ~ Analytics

From great data come great responsibility. We are working hard to give you the most interesting KPIs you have ever watched.



7 ~ Export data

Export your data and explore them within your favourite tool.
