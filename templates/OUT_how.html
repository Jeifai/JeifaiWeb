Jefai is cool and simple.

Three things to remember.

1 ~ Targets

A target is a company you decide to monitor. Whether you target a big corporation or a small startup, we track their career page. You can choose a target from a list of existing ones, or in alternative ask a new one. In such a case, our team evaluates you request and write you as soon as possible.

2 ~ Keywords

A keyword is a word presents in a job published by some of your targets. Imagine it as the word that must be present on the job title. For example, an analytical profile would add "data", while a marketer would add "marketing", and so on.
Pretty simple right?

3 ~ Matches

Let's connect the dots.
keyword + target = match.
In this section you can find all your results. Anyhow, you will be receiving an alert whenever a match happens. The goal is to react as fast as possible to a match in order to be the first on the list.

Now you know (almost) everything about us. Ready to have a go?
